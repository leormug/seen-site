Been told “it’s all in your head” one too many times?

There’s something that changes everything.

Walk into appointments with a summary that makes doctors sit up and pay attention. It sounds simple—but it’s transformative. Hand your doctor a clear, organized summary before the appointment starts. Your timeline, symptoms, treatments, and why you’re there—all on one page they can scan in under a minute. Easy to read, impossible to ignore.

Three steps to better appointments

1. Tell your story once
Fill in the guided form. SEEN walks you through what doctors need to know.

2. Print and hand it over
Present your one-page summary at the start of your appointment.

3. Update for each visit
Add new symptoms, test results, and treatments over time.

Your medical story stays yours

Everything stays on your device. No cloud, no servers, no accounts. Works completely offline. Zero tracking or data collection.

SEEN helps you organize information for your medical team. It does not replace professional medical advice.

Use SEEN on any device

iPhone, iPad, Android, Windows, Mac — install SEEN with one tap.

Open SEEN
  • iPhone / iPad: Open SEEN, then tap the Share → “Add to Home Screen”.
  • Android: Open SEEN, then tap “Install app” when prompted.
  • Mac / Windows: Open SEEN, then click “Install SEEN” in your browser bar.
