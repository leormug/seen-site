SEEN: Be heard. Be believed.

Been told “it’s all in your head” one too many times?
Tired of doctors who don’t listen?
Always forget to mention what matters most?

There’s something that changes everything.

Walk into appointments with a summary that makes doctors sit up and pay attention. It sounds simple—but it’s transformative. Hand your doctor a clear, organized summary before the appointment starts. Your timeline, symptoms, treatments, and why you’re there—all on one page they can scan in under a minute. Easy to read, impossible to ignore.

Three steps to better appointments

1. Tell your story once
Fill in the guided form. SEEN walks you through what doctors need to know.

2. Print and hand it over
Present your one-page summary at the start of your appointment.

3. Update for each visit
Add new symptoms, test results, and treatments over time.

Your medical story stays yours

Everything stays on your device. No cloud, no servers, no accounts. Works completely offline. Zero tracking or data collection.

Mac Instructions

  1. Download the .dmg file using the link above.
  2. Open the downloaded file and drag SEEN into your Applications folder.
  3. If macOS blocks it:
    • Open System Settings → Privacy & Security.
    • Find “SEEN was blocked…” and click Open Anyway.
  4. Open SEEN from your Applications folder. All data stays local.

Windows Instructions

  1. Download the .exe installer using the link above.
  2. If Chrome warns “Not commonly downloaded,” click Keep.
  3. If Windows SmartScreen appears:
    • Click More info → Run anyway.
  4. Finish the installer. Open SEEN from your desktop or Start menu.

Phone & Tablet Instructions (iOS & Android)

  1. Open your browser and go to:
    https://leormug.github.io/seen-site/app/
  2. Add SEEN to your home screen:
    • iPhone/iPad: Share → Add to Home Screen.
    • Android: Menu (⋮) → Install App or Add to Home Screen.
  3. Open SEEN from your home screen. It behaves like a native app and keeps all data on your device.

Using SEEN

  • Create your password on first launch.
  • Enter symptoms, history, medications, tests, and visit reasons.
  • Print a clean one-page summary for your doctor.
  • Your data stays on your device even when updating SEEN.
