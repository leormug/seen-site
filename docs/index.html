Three steps to better appointments

1. Tell your story once
Fill in the guided form. SEEN walks you through what doctors need to know—your symptoms, timeline, treatments, and how it all affects your life.

2. Print and hand it over
Print the one-page summary and bring it to your visit. Give it to your doctor at the start so they can read your full story before the appointment begins.

3. Update for each visit
After the appointment, note what changed—new symptoms, medications, test results. Next time, you’re ready with an updated version.

Your medical story stays yours

Everything stays on your device. No cloud, no servers, no accounts. Works completely offline. You control what to print, share, or delete. Zero tracking or data collection.

SEEN helps you organize information for your medical team. It does not replace professional medical advice.
